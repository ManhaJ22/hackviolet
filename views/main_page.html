Pregnancy Statistics

According to BlueCross BlueShield, 16% of women have complications in their pregnancy. Of the 16%, 14% did not get prenatal care in their first trimester. One third of the 16% also received less than the recommend number of prenatal visits; a quarter of the women had complications during childbirth. With an easy way to track doctors appointment, how their baby should be growing, and ways to monitor how your body should react to pregnancy they can have a safer pregnancy.
